CRO Audit Report — arcaridentallab.com

Date: March 24, 2026  |  Industry: Dental Lab (B2B)  |  Benchmark CR: 3–5% (B2B Lead Gen)
Estimated Improvement Potential: 40–80% lift with recommended changes

Critical Issues (Fix Immediately)

1. No Clear Primary Conversion Goal

The page tries to do everything: starter kit requests, case submissions, Rx downloads, DDX login, newsletter signup. A new dentist visiting has no clear path.

Fix: Define ONE primary CTA ("Request a Starter Kit" for new dentists) and make everything else secondary. Remove "Learn More" from the hero entirely.

Expected impact: +15–25% conversion lift

2. Headline "Make It Arcari!" Says Nothing

A first-time visitor has no idea what this means or why they should care. It's a slogan, not a value proposition. Fails the 5-second test.

Fix: Replace with benefit-driven headline:

  • "Precise Restorations. On Time. Every Time."
  • "The Full-Service Dental Lab That 500+ Practices Trust for Quality & Fit"

Sub-copy: "From PFMs to full-arch implants — get your starter kit and experience Arcari's TQM quality guarantee."

Expected impact: +20–30% engagement lift

3. Zero Social Proof Above the Fold

No customer count, no testimonials, no logos, no ratings. For a B2B service where trust is everything, this is a major gap.

Fix: Add directly below the hero:

  • "Trusted by [X]+ dental practices across [region]"
  • 3–5 dentist testimonials with photo, name, practice name
  • Years in business badge

Expected impact: +15–20% trust and CR lift

High-Impact Opportunities

4. No Testimonials Anywhere on the Page

This is a B2B service where dentists are trusting you with their patients' restorations. Zero voice-of-customer content is a serious conversion killer.

Fix: Add 3 testimonial cards after the product grid. Each should include: dentist photo, full name, practice name, specific quote about quality/fit/turnaround. Address the top objections: "Will the fit be right?" "Will it arrive on time?" "Is the quality consistent?"

5. About Section is Company-Focused, Not Customer-Focused

Current: "Arcari Dental Laboratories, located in Wakefield, MA…" — nobody cares where you're located first. The copy talks about the company, not the dentist's problems.

Fix — Rewrite to lead with the dentist's pain:

"Tired of remakes and adjustments? Every Arcari restoration passes through our TQM quality checkpoints — so what arrives at your chair fits the first time. Your patients get better outcomes. You get time back in your schedule."

6. Product Cards Have No CTAs

Six product cards with descriptions but no buttons. Dead ends. Visitors read about implants and then… what?

Fix: Add a CTA button to each card: "View Implant Options →" linking to the product detail page. Or at minimum: "Learn More →"

7. No "How It Works" Section

For a new dentist, the process of partnering with a lab is unclear. How do I send a case? What's the turnaround? How do I get started?

Fix: Add a 3-step section:

  1. Request Your Starter Kit — We send Rx forms, case boxes, and your fee schedule
  2. Send Your Case — Local pickup, UPS shipping, or digital impression upload
  3. Receive Quality Restorations — TQM-checked, on time, every time

8. No Pricing Transparency

No fee schedule visible. "Request Fee Schedule" is buried in the Contact dropdown. Dentists comparison-shopping will bounce.

Fix: Either show starting prices or make the fee schedule downloadable with one click from the hero area. Even "Starting at $XX per unit" builds confidence.

9. Images Not Optimized

PNGs at 2x resolution, no WebP, no lazy loading. This directly impacts load time and mobile experience.

Fix: Convert all images to WebP, add loading="lazy" to below-fold images. Target LCP under 2.5s.

Detailed Recommendations by Section

Hero Section

Current: "Make It Arcari!" + "Your Full-Service Dental Laboratory" + two buttons

Recommended: Benefit headline + specific sub-copy + single CTA + social proof line. Add a hero image showing a dentist examining a high-quality restoration (human connection).

Navigation

Too many top-level items for a landing page. If running paid traffic, consider a nav-free landing page variant (typically +10–15% CR lift). "DDX Login" should be a small utility link, not competing with acquisition CTAs.

Product Grid

Good content, but purely informational. Each card needs a CTA. Consider adding a "Most Popular" badge to your highest-margin product.

Partnership / Starter Kit Section

This is your real conversion section but it's buried mid-page. Move this concept above the product grid, or duplicate the CTA earlier.

Footer

Solid with contact info, hours, and Rx forms. Add a final CTA block before the footer: "Ready to experience the Arcari difference? Get your free starter kit today." + button.

Missing Elements

  • No guarantee language — Add: "Every restoration backed by our TQM quality guarantee"
  • No urgency/scarcity — Consider: "Free starter kit includes your first case box and Rx forms"
  • No video — A 60-second lab tour video would dramatically increase trust
  • No case studies / before-and-after gallery on the homepage (it exists in Resources but isn't surfaced)

Bottom line: The site looks professional but converts like a brochure. It tells visitors what Arcari does but never answers why should I switch labs or what happens if I'm not satisfied. The biggest wins are: (1) benefit-driven headline, (2) social proof, and (3) a single clear conversion path for new dentists.
